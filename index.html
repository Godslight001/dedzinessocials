Overall Social Media Strategy

Here you'll find the overarching strategy for De'Dzine's social media presence. These points guide the content creation process to maximize engagement and brand growth.

Engagement: Focus on behind-the-scenes, DIY inspiration, customer spotlights, polls, and interactive questions.

Google Ranking: Utilize relevant keywords in captions (e.g., "recycled tire furniture India," "eco-friendly home decor," "sustainable seating solutions," "upcycled tire art"), encourage website visits, and promote Google My Business reviews.

Visuals: High-quality photos and videos are crucial. Show the transformation process, the finished products in beautiful settings, and the craftsmanship.

Hashtags: Mix of broad, niche, and trending hashtags. Always include #DeDzine, #RecycledTires, #UpcycledFurniture, #SustainableLiving, #MadeInIndia.

Call to Action (CTA): Clear and concise CTAs in every post (e.g., "Shop now," "Learn more," "Tag a friend," "Visit our website").

Key Posting Tips

This section provides essential tips and best practices to ensure your social media efforts are effective. Refer to these guidelines when creating and publishing content.

  • High-Quality Visuals: Invest in good lighting and a decent camera. Blurry or poorly lit images will not engage.
  • Consistency: Stick to your 4 posts a week. Algorithms reward consistency.
  • Engagement is Key: Respond to every comment and DM promptly. Ask questions in your captions.
  • Hashtag Strategy: Research trending and relevant hashtags. Use a mix of broad, niche, and location-specific hashtags.
  • Cross-Promotion: Share your social media posts on your website, email newsletters, and other platforms.
  • Analytics: Regularly review your social media analytics to see what's performing well and adjust your strategy accordingly.
  • Google My Business: Actively encourage customers to leave reviews on your Google My Business profile. This is crucial for local SEO.
  • Website Integration: Ensure your website is linked in your bio and prominently featured in your posts. Drive traffic to product pages.
  • Community Building: Create a sense of community around sustainable living and unique home decor.
